Type of Ownership vs. Population, Income and Poverty Count

Observations:

1) No obvious correlation b/w # of government ownership vs. population or income or poverty count

2) There's a mild correlation shown between # of non profit ownership: The higher the poverty count or average population, the higher # of non profit ownership.

3) There's a mild correlation shown between # of proprietary ownership: The higher the poverty count or average population, the higher # of proprietary ownership.
